# 🌐 OSINT Links by Country – Latin America

A source of links to conduct OSINT investigations from sources within a specific country including different online services and government webpages.

This project compiles online resources such as government databases, judicial portals, corporate registries, and utilities that are useful for Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) research.

> ✅ Starting with **Ecuador**, this list will be expanded to include as many Latin American countries as possible.

## 🇪🇨 Ecuador

- [Superintendencia de Compañías – Sector Societario](https://appscvsmovil.supercias.gob.ec/PortalInformacion/sector_societario.html)  
  Company information, legal representatives.
- [SRI – Facturador Electrónico](https://facturadorsri.sri.gob.ec/portal-facturadorsri-internet/pages/comprobantes/factura/Factura.html)  
  Check issued electronic invoices.
- [Consulta Propietario Vehículo](https://consultasecuador.com/en-linea/transito/consultar-propietario-vehiculo)  
  Search for vehicle owner information.
- [Procesos Judiciales](https://procesosjudiciales.funcionjudicial.gob.ec/busqueda-filtros)  
  Judicial case lookup.
- [CNEL – Consulta Cuenta](https://serviciosenlinea.cnelep.gob.ec/consulta-cuen/)  
  Electricity account lookup.
- [CNT – Guía Telefónica](https://www.micnt.com.ec/cntapp/guia104/php/guia_cntat.php?hflagsubmit=0&cmbcriterio=3)  
  Telephone directory.
- [Aguapen – Consulta de Servicios](http://www.aguapen.gob.ec/aguapenep/consulta/)  
  Water service query.
- [ATM Guayaquil – Consulta Cliente](https://consultas.atm.gob.ec/PortalWEB/paginas/clientes/clp_criterio_consulta.jsp)  
  Vehicle and transport-related data.

- [DNRPA – Vehicle Registry](https://www.dnrpa.gov.ar/)
